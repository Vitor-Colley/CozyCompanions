COZY COMPANIONS is recognised as Ireland's leading animal welfare charity.
Our mission is that through education, legislation and ongoing support, we can prevent cruelity to all animals.
We believe that man has a responsibility to care for the earth and all creatures on it, and that each creature has an intrinsic value, entirely independent of its value to man, and should be respected and protected.
We believe that all animals kept by, or under the control of, humans must be kept in circumstances appropriate to their species.
